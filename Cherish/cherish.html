    About Me

    I am a third-year Fine Arts student concentrating in Painting and Drawing at the University of North Florida. The artistic approach to my work canvary from where my style becomes more two-dimensional and cartoon-like to being three-dimensional and more realistic in form. My process involves a combination of traditional and digital mediums. I am constantly experimenting with different ways to express my ideas and create something new. I am inspired to explore different media and techniques to create unique works of art.

    In the past few years, I have developed a strong preference for working digitally on my laptop and graphics tablet as I develop my skills in rendering and drawing more smoothly, whether I'm creating digital artworks for friends, family, and loved ones. Additionally, I enjoy working in traditional mediums (pen and ink) to accentuate my linework and texture. I greatly love pop culture such as Japanese anime, manga, and Korean dramas/pop songs. I use these as inspiration for my work, and I also enjoy creating art that reflects the themes of these stories. As an artist, I strive to create pieces that evoke emotion and bring joy to those who see them.
